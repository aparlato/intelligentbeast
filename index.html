Intelligent Beast is the creation of John Saag, a former VP of Marketing Production at Discovery Networks, and John Parlato, an award-winning writer/creative director.

JOHN P

John is a former Executive Creative Director with Doner, one of the country’s largest independent ad agencies. He invented the iconic “Counting Sheep” for Serta Mattresses, and was part of the team that created “Zoom-Zoom” for Mazda, and Mr. Six, the dancing octogenarian, for Six Flags Amusement Parks. His work has received two Cannes Lions, Andy Awards, Clios, One Show, and, in 2007, the Counting Sheep were voted into the Advertising Hall of Fame.

JOHN S

Most recently, John was VP of Operations for Discovery’s In-House Agency, where he managed the promotional broadcast and digital efforts for all Discovery networks. He oversaw production on a number of award-winning campaigns, including “Shark Week” and "Mythbusters." Prior to that, he was head of production at several ad agencies. During his career he has won a Cannes Gold Lion, Clio and One Show awards, and earned numerous other industry accolades.
